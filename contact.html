Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and our services

What is your response time for inquiries?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For charter flight requests and aircraft on ground (AOG) maintenance emergencies, we respond within 2 hours, 24/7.

Do you offer international charter services?

Yes, we provide international private charter services to over 50 global destinations. Our flight planning team handles all international clearances, customs, and logistics to ensure seamless international travel.

What are the requirements for flight training?

Basic requirements include being at least 16 years old to solo (17 for license), able to read, speak, and understand English, and passing an FAA medical examination. No previous flight experience is necessary for our PPL program.

How do I schedule aircraft maintenance?

You can schedule maintenance by calling our maintenance hotline at +1 (555) 123-4570 or emailing maintenance@skyboundvision.com. For AOG situations, call our 24/7 emergency line for immediate assistance.

Do you offer financing for flight training?

Yes, we offer several financing options for our flight training programs, including payment plans, loans through aviation financing partners, and veterans benefits for eligible candidates. Contact our training department for details.
