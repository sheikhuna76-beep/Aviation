Our Aviation Legacy

For over 25 years, Sky-Bound Vision has been at the forefront of aviation excellence, setting industry standards and helping clients reach new heights.

Explore Our Journey

Pioneers in Aviation Excellence

Founded in 1995 by Captain Robert Sterling, Sky-Bound Vision began as a small flight training school with a single Cessna 172 and a vision to revolutionize aviation education.

Today, we've grown into a comprehensive aviation solutions provider, offering everything from private charter flights to advanced pilot training and aircraft maintenance.

Our commitment to safety, innovation, and exceptional service has earned us ICAO certification and recognition as a leader in the aviation industry.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our 25+ year history of aviation excellence

1995

Sky-Bound Vision Founded

Captain Robert Sterling establishes Sky-Bound Vision with a single aircraft and a vision to revolutionize flight training.

2002

ICAO Certification

Achieved ICAO certification, establishing international standards for safety and operational excellence.

2008

Charter Services Launch

Expanded operations to include premium private charter services for business and leisure travel.

2015

State-of-the-Art Facility

Opened our new headquarters with advanced flight simulators and maintenance facilities.

2020

10,000th Pilot Trained

2023

Global Expansion

Established partnerships with aviation authorities in 50+ countries, expanding our global reach.
